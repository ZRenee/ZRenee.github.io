Research Experience

  • 2020.09-present
    Participant

    Event-based Semantic Segmentation with Spiking Neural Networks through Hierarchical Search and Spatially-Adaptive Modulation
      Personal contribution: experiment implementation and paper writing.
      We adopt spike-based differentiable hierarchical search to construct SNNs with full spiking computation for event-based semantic segmentation based on sparse input event streams. In addition, we have developed a general-purpose learning-based spiking spatially-adaptive modulation (SSAM) method to enhance event representation with augmented input to further improve prediction quality.
      Our SNNs achieve new state-of-the-art performance both on the DDD17 dataset and benchmark image datasets(in the field of SNN) of semantic segmentation with only one time-step for convergence, smaller network size and much lower computation cost.
